1.              What makes video games so enticing and entertaining? This question is asked quite a lot by people who do not play video games. And there are a lot of different answers to this broad question; to some people it’s an escape from their lives and all the bad things going on in the world, to others a way to connect to friends and meet new people. For me video games are fun because I'm lucky enough to be able to play with my family and more specifically my cousins, without them I would barely play or maybe not even play at all.                                                 Gamequitters.com Summary: Games are a fun thing to do for lots of people, but there are many different reasons why people find them fun. For example one person might find video games fun because they enjoy the feeling of completing all the game has to offer, while another person finds video games fun because they get to do whatever they want without having to be required to do something that they don't find fun.
