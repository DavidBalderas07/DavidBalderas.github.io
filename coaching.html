Noob to Mid-level Mechanics

This website is to show you mechanics that can help you reach the next rank. Weather your a noob or veteran these are some solid mechanics to improve your game play. During this phase of the game mechanics are pretty easy to pick but hard to master.

1.Powersliding

Powersliding is a simple mechanic that helps with hard turns and conserving momentum. To power slide you press your drift button (not for too long) and turn your stick left or right.

2. Half- Flipping

3.Dribbling

Knowing this mechanic will put you a step ahead of people in the lower ranks. Dribbling is where you have to balance the ball. The best way to do this is to try to stay in the center of the ball marker.

4.Shadow Defence

Shadow defense is where you are on the same side of the field as your opponent and react off their movements. This mechanic is essential to improving your defense.

5. Fast ariel

Fast Ariel is a very good mechanic to speed up your Ariel and beat your opponent to the ball. How to do a fast Ariel: left stick down, boost and jump, release stick, boost and jump, keep boosting.
